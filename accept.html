Frequently Asked Questions

We understand this is a stressful time for students looking forward for their studies after completing their senior secondary or graduation. The closure of all educational institutes due to COVID-19 lockdown has resulted in exam delays and is affecting both school and college students around the world, and there is still some uncertainty about the extent of disruption before the situation gets normal.

We are closely monitoring the on-going COVID-19 pandemic situation and have taken several contingency measures in order to facilitate prospective students in joining higher studies at Lovely Professional University. The updated information related to admission process will be available on our website www.lpu.in.
